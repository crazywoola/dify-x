Challenges & friction

Ecosystem challenges
  • Maintainer sustainability and incentives
  • Community diversity and scale limits
  • Contribution barriers and collaboration efficiency
Organization challenges
  • Low enterprise participation, missing OSPOs
  • Limited policy and funding support
  • Complex compliance and IP governance
Key question: how to upgrade passion-driven efforts into long-term mechanisms
Summarize core challenges: sustainability, enterprise participation, policy resources, compliance.

Observations & recommendations

  • Open source is public infrastructure for innovation and digital governance in Hong Kong
  • Build OSPOs and community funds to grow enterprise and startup participation
  • Use open data and standardized interfaces to enable cross-agency collaboration
  • Create two-way contribution channels with global foundations and regional communities
  • Embed open-source education in university curricula and professional training
Why open source matters: it makes collaboration sustainable and value scalable
